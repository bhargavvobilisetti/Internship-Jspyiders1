📍 Andhra Pradesh - An Overview

Andhra Pradesh is located in the southeastern part of India. Known for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments, and beautiful landscapes, it is the seventh-largest state in India. The state’s economy is based on agriculture, industry, and services, and it is a major producer of rice. The official language is Telugu. Amaravati is the legislative capital, and Visakhapatnam is the largest city and financial capital.

🌆 Famous Tourist Places in Andhra Pradesh

1. Tirupati

Home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

2. Araku Valley

A scenic hill station known for coffee plantations, waterfalls, and tribal culture.

3. Lepakshi

Known for its hanging pillar and beautiful architecture from the Vijayanagara era.
