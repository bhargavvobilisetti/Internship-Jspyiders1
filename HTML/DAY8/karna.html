📍 Karnataka - An Overview

Karnataka is located in the southwestern region of India and is known for its rich heritage, vibrant culture, and strong IT industry. Bengaluru, its capital city, is known as the “Silicon Valley of India”. The state is also famous for its beautiful landscapes, ancient temples, wildlife sanctuaries, and world heritage sites like Hampi and Pattadakal.

🌆 Famous Tourist Places in Karnataka

1. Bengaluru

The IT capital of India, known for parks, nightlife, and tech hubs. Also called the Garden City of India.

2. Mysore

Famous for the Mysore Palace, Dasara Festival, and traditional silk sarees.

3. Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire and unique stone structures.

4. Coorg

A picturesque hill station also called the “Scotland of India”, famous for coffee plantations and waterfalls.
