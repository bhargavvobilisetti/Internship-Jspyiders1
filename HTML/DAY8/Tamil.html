ğŸ“ Tamil Nadu - An Overview

Tamil Nadu is a state in the southern part of India, known for its ancient Dravidian-style temples, classical music and dance, and deep-rooted culture. It is one of the most industrialized and urbanized states in the country. The capital city, Chennai, is a major hub for education, health care, and IT. The official language is Tamil, one of the oldest surviving classical languages in the world.
