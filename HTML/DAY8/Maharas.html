ğŸ“ Maharashtra - An Overview

Maharashtra is a state located in the western part of India. It is the second-most populous state and home to the financial capital of India, Mumbai. Known for its historical forts, UNESCO World Heritage sites, film industry (Bollywood), and diverse landscapes, Maharashtra is a blend of heritage and modernity. The official language is Marathi.
